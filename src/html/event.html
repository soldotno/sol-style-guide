Something happened; it's an event

On our event pages, we collect stories about a specific thing. A "thing" can be this or that: Breaking news, an anticipated sports event, a reoccuring topic (Danger: Ticks!) or something completely different.

In its essence, the intrinsic nature of what we call an event is that comprises a collection of news stories.

On this page, we have collected a few different kinds of content that often occur on our event pages.
